Why visit
Dandeli has it all
🏞️
Untouched nature
Home to the Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary and Anshi National Park — one of Karnataka's richest biodiversity zones.

India's best river rafting
The Kali River offers thrilling Grade 3–4 rapids, making it a top destination for adventure seekers across India.

Affordable & close
Just 5 hours from Goa, Bangalore, and Pune — perfect for a quick long weekend without burning your wallet.
